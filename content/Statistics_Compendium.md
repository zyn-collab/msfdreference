# Statistics Compendium

## A Statistical Reference for Social Policy Analysis, Treaty Body Reporting, and Evidence-Based Decision-Making

---

## Overview

This chapter consolidates all available quantitative data relevant to social policy in the Maldives into a single statistical reference. It draws from national censuses (2006, 2014, 2022), Household Income and Expenditure Surveys (HIES 2016, 2019), Demographic and Health Surveys (DHS 2009, 2016-17), WHO STEPS surveys, Global School-based Student Health Surveys (GSHS 2009, 2014), the Statistical Yearbooks of Maldives (2022, 2023, 2024), administrative data from government agencies, police crime statistics, and international databases (World Bank, WHO, UNICEF, ILO).

Statistics are organised into 15 thematic domains aligned with treaty body reporting requirements under the Convention on the Rights of the Child (CRC), Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination against Women (CEDAW), and Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (CRPD). Where time-series data exists, historical trends are presented to support trajectory analysis.
