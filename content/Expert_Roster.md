# Expert Roster

This section is reserved for the Ministry's roster of subject-matter experts and valuable sources across key policy domains. Each entry will include the expert's name, area of specialisation, a brief summary of their expertise and contributions, and contact details where available.

The roster is intended to serve as a quick-reference guide for Ministry staff seeking authoritative input on specific topics – whether for policy drafting, programme design, stakeholder consultation, or technical review. Domains covered will include child protection, gender-based violence, disability rights, elderly care, social protection, mental health, substance abuse, legal reform, and development cooperation.

Entries will be added and updated as the Ministry identifies and engages with relevant experts through its ongoing policy work.
