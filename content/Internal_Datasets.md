# Datasets

---

This section hosts clean, correctly structured copies of non-confidential ministry datasets and spreadsheets available for download.

Any department within the Ministry can submit their data files and spreadsheets to the Policy Department, which will clean, validate, and restructure them into properly formatted, downloadable datasets. Once processed, they are made available here for ministry-wide use.

Selected datasets that meet quality and sensitivity thresholds may also be released publicly as open data under open government protocols, allowing researchers, development partners, and other stakeholders to access and use them.

Upon request, the Policy Department will also develop data analyses, graphs, and visualizations from these datasets. Analyses and visualizations that receive approval are added to the Statistics Compendium and the Graphs & Charts Library, making them easy to find and reuse across the ministry.
